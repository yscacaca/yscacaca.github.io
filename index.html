About

Bio

I am a PhD student in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, supervised by Prof. Tarek F. Abdelzaher. I got my B.S. degree from Shanghi Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Xinbing Wang.

Research Interest

My research interest is bringing Deep Learning techniques into Internet of Things (IoT). I am especially interested in the system efficiency, reliability, semi-supervision, and related applications of deep learning enabled IoT. I am also interested in social and crowd sensing.
